In the video and this documentation we are focusing on the yoga solution of eye strain. Lets get into a deep dive about the asana.

Trataka is a yogic practice that involves gazing at a single point or object to improve concentration, focus, eyestrain and mental clarity. The word "Trataka" comes from the Sanskrit word "trāṭaka," which means "to gaze" or "to look steadily."
