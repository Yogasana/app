In the video and this documentation we are focusing on the yoga solution of indigestion. Lets get into a deep dive about padmastask sanyukt asana.

Padmastask Asana is a mixture of bhujagasana with just lifting the legs additionally.

To do padmastask asana first lie on the floor on your stomach and first start lifting your back with the help of your hands like in bhujangasana then start to lifting your legs and try to touch your head.
