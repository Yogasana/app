Indigestion

In the documentation below Jatin will be talking in depth about the diet chart to follow if you are suffering from indigestion.

Documentation

In this documentation we are focusing on the diet chart of indigestion. Lets get into a deep dive about it.

You must not eat junk food at any cost. You also should not eat fruits such as grapes, banana and peach & and vegetables like capsicum and brinjal.

You should eat light food such as dalia and kichdi. If it is possible please also avoid chapatis or eat very thin chapatis.
