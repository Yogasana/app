In this documentation we are focusing on the diet chart of diabetes. Lets get into a deep dive about it.

Ayurveda recommends avoiding meats as they may increase inflammation. Rice and wheat are heavy and difficult to break down. Light grains like millet can be eaten.

Eating foods that are light, dry, and warm and avoiding foods that are oily, cold, and heavy. Fruits such as apples, pomegranates, and berries can be eaten.
