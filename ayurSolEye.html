In this documentation we are focusing on the ayurvedic solution of eye strain. Lets get into a deep dive about triphala Churna.

Aniseed (Saunf) water is a remedy to reduce eye strain. Boil aniseeds in water, let it cool, and use it as an eye wash. It can help soothe and refresh the eyes.

Triphala is an herbal combination made from three fruits: amla (Indian gooseberry), haritaki, and bibhitaki. It is used to make an eye wash solution. To prepare, soak Triphala powder in water overnight and strain it the next morning. Use this solution as an eye wash to relieve eye strain and refresh tired eyes.
